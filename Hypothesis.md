# MOVE 2: THE HYPOTHESIS

*(Timestamp = 2:06pm)*

## The Claim

A learner who can correctly define a systems-thinking concept may still struggle to derive or apply it when faced with a sharp follow-up question.

This tool identifies the exact point where genuine understanding gives way to memorized definitions, helping learners bridge that gap and build true conceptual mastery.

## Result That Proves Me Right

1 or 2 of the 2 learners fail the initial derivation attempt but successfully derive the concept after the intervention.

## Result That Means No Problem Exists

Both learners are able to derive the concept correctly before the intervention, indicating no observable derivation gap.

## Kill Number

Invalidates the hypothesis if neither learner demonstrates a meaningful gap between defining and deriving the concept, indicating the core assumption is incorrect.
